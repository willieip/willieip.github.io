Willie Ip is a designer and art director based in New York City. More info +
He currently works at Instagram and Threads within the Product Brand Studio, where he focuses on editorial and branded product experiences. He also teaches Graphic Design I & II at the School of Visual Arts.

Willie received his BFA in Graphic Design from Purchase College, School of Art and Design, and is open to select projects.
