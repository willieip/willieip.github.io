Willie Ip is a New York City–based graphic designer and art director. He currently works at Instagram and Threads within the Product Brand Studio, where he focuses on editorial and branded product experiences. He also teaches Graphic Design I & II at the School of Visual Arts. Willie received his BFA in Graphic Design from Purchase College, School of Art and Design, and is open to select projects.
